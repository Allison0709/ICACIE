Registration Details

Every accepted article should be registered by at least one author to appear in the proceedings. The acceptance list of articles will be announced on the website and the authors will be intimated regarding the acceptance through e-mail. The details about events and dates of registration are given below:

Registration and Camera-ready submission of the accepted articles 15th July 2023
Author registration with late fees of Rs 3000 (Mauritian authors) or $90 (foreign authors) 16th July 2023 - 25th July 2023

Registration includes ALL the following events to be completed by the author’s aforementioned deadline.

Registration fees Details

Author Registration

To ensure the publication of a paper in the Proceedings, at least one author has to register by submitting a normal registration fee within the deadline as indicated below.


Category Mauritian(Rs) Foreigner($)
Author-Students 7000 250
Author-Academician 8000 300
Author-Industry Affiliate 10000 400

Note:

 All bank charges must be borne by the payer.

The normal registration fee covers publication charges and lunch. The over-length charges beyond 10 pages (in LNNS format) will be added to the Normal registration fees to calculate the actual registration fee to be paid by the authors. The over-length charge is Rs. 1500(for Mauritian authors) or $25 (for foreign authors) per extra page for a maximum of four extra pages.

Cancellation

If any author/listener wants to cancel his or her registration, he must write a mail to icacie2023@udm.ac.mu on or before 25-JUL-2022 with the subject “Cancellation of Registration – ICACIE XXX”. A processing fee of MRU Rs 2500 will be deducted from the paid registration fee and the rest amount will be returned to the author/listener on 7-Sep-2023 No request for authorship cancellation can be made after 25-JUL-2023.
